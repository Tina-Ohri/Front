Sched Track

The application helps you to track the schedule of a teacher in college and helps you find him/her in need.
Just login and explore the schedules of teachers, and find them where they are to submit your assignments, projects in time.
You can also view your time table and keep a track of your monthly attendance.

What teachers have to do?

You just have to sumbit your time table when the semester starts, and mark your attendance everyday. A student will be able to find you in college in case of need, based on the data the teacher provides. A student will be able to submit all projects and assignments on time.

What students have to do?

You just have to sumbit your attendance everyday. You will be able to find a teacher in college in case of need, based on the data the teacher provides. You will be able to submit all projects and assignments on time.
